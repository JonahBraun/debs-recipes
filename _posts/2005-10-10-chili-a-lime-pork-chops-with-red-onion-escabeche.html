--- layout: recipe title: Chili & Lime Pork Chops with Red Onion Escabeche category: pork --- A great mexican dish with an extra kick that is a nice change from stereo typical burritos.
 1/4 c chili powder
2 large tomatos chopped
1 tbs crushed coriander
4 cloves garlic, sliced
1 tsp salt
 1/4 c fresh lime juice
3 lbs pork chops


Red Onion Escabeche:

 2 large carrots, cut in matchsticks
2 tbs oil
1 large red onion, chopped
1/2 c chopped cilantro
1/2 c fresh lime juice
 1 tsp salt


Brown pork chops.  Mix chili powder, coriander and salt.  Add to chops along with tomatoes, garlic and lime juice.  Simmer until tender.

Meanwhile prepare escabeche.  Combine carrots, red onion, lime juice, oil, cilantro and salt, mixing well.

Serve on corn tortillas with sour cream, salsa, Queso fresco (Mexican style cheese) and lime wedges.
