--- layout: recipe title: Tapioca Custard Pudding category: desserts ---

3/4 c tapioca

1 1/2 c milk

2 beaten egg yolks

2 beaten egg whites

1/3 c sugar

1/8 tsp salt

1/2 tsp vanilla

 

Soak tapioca in enough cold water to cover until water is absorbed.  Add milk and cook in double boiler until tapioca is transparent.  To the beaten yolks, sugar and salt, add a little of the hot mixture.  Return all to double boiler and cook 3 minutes.  Cool slightly.  Fold in well beaten whites, flavor and serve.
