--- layout: recipe title: Roquefort Pear Salad category: salads --- Most people say this is the best salad they have ever eaten!
1 head leaf lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
1/3 cup olive oil
3 pears - cored and chopped
3 tbs red wine vinegar
5 oz. Roquefort (or any blue) cheese, crumbled
1 1/2 tsp white sugar
1 avacodo, diced
1 1/2 tsp prepared mustard (dijon is nice)
 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
1 clove garlic, chopped
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup pecans
fresh ground black pepper to taste

1.  In a skillet over medium heat, stir 1/4 cup of sugar together with the pecans.  Continue stirring gently until sugar has melted and carmelized the pecans.  Transfer nuts onto waxed paper.  Allow to cool and break into pieces.

2.  For the dressing, blend oil, vinegar, 1 1/2 tsps sugar, mustard, chopped garlic, salt, and pepper.

3.  In a large serving bowl, layer lettuce, pears, blue cheese, avacado, and green onions.  Pour dressing over salad, sprinkle with pecans and serve.
