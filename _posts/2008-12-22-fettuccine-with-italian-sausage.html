--- layout: recipe title: Fettuccine with Italian Sausage category: beef ---
375 g fettuccine
1 jar sweet pepper & onion pasta sauce
250 g Italian sausage
1/2 each green and yellow peppers, in strips
1 small onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbs parsley
 
Cook fettuccine. Remove sausage from casings.  Cut into pieces and brown.  Add peppers, onion, garlic and parsley.  Saute 4 minutes.  Stir in pasta sauce and simmer 5 minutes.  Spoon over fettuccine.
