--- layout: recipe title: Thai Chicken category: poultry --- The quick version.
 1 tbs oil
1 lb boneless chicken
10 oz chicken broth
2 tbs liquid honey
2 tbs soy sauce
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 tbs corn starch
1 tsp ground ginger or 2 tsp fresh
2 green onions
 2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 red pepper, cut into strips
 1/2 tsp hot sauce

In skillet, heat oil; saute chicken until golden.  Remove chicken; set aside and keep warm.  In clean skillet, combine broth, honey and soysauce.  Wisk in peanut butter, corn starch, hot sauce and ginger. Add green onions and garlic; cook stirring constantly over low heat until blended and smooth.  Add red pepper and reserved chicken; cook and stir until just bubbly and thickened.
