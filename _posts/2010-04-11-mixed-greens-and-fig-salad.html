--- layout: recipe title: Mixed Greens and Fig Salad category: salads --- Orange vinaigrette:
Whisk together:
juice of 1 orange
1 tbsp sweet white wine
3 tbsp red wine vinegar
3 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
2 tbsp olive oil

Salad:

2 regular oranges
1 cup pecans
1 cup fresh figs or dried calimyrna figs, sliced into thin wedges
1/4 cup red onion finely sliced
one large package of mixed greens
