--- layout: recipe title: Banana Bread category: desserts --- The best banana bread I've come across yet, and is very moist!
1 1/2 c flour
 1 egg
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 c mashed bananas
1/4 tsp baking soda
 3/4 c sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
 1/4 c cooking oil


Mix alltogether.  Pour batter into greased loaf pan.  Bake at 350 F for 50-55 minutes.
