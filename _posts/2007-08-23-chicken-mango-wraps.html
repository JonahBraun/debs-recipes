--- layout: recipe title: Chicken Mango Wraps category: poultry --- Soooo easy and delicious. 
1 tbs Jerk sesoning  (substitute other hot sauce if desired)
2 halves chicken breasts
1 mango, diced
1/4 cup mayonaise
1 tbs lime juice
1 tbs oil
1 cup shredded lettuce
half red pepper
1/4 tsp grated lime peel
tortillas

Whisk jerk with oil.  Cut chicken into strips and toss with oil and jerk.  Broil or stir fry chicken until done.  Toss cooked chicken with all the other ingredients except tortilla shells.   Wrap the mixture in the shells.
