--- layout: recipe title: Sweet Potato and Zucchini Bake category: sides --- Another tasty infusion of spices, this side dish works well with any meat dish, especially asian.
 2  cups sweet potato cut into 1" cubes
1 tbs hot curry paste
1 medium zucchini , sliced into 1/2" rounds
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbs olive oil
 freshly ground pepper
1 tbs unsalted butter
 1 tbs Chipotle barbecue sauce
              
Melt butter and mix with oil and spices in 9" baking pan.  Add sweet potato and zucchini, stirring to coat.  Bake in 350 degree oven,  for approximately 30 minutes or until sweet potoato is tender.  Stir once or twice while baking.
