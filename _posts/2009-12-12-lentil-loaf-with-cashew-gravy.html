For loaf:

1/2 cup red lentils
1/2 cup brown rice or bulger
1/3 cup dahl

2 tbs olive oil
4 cloves minced garlic
2 tbs fresh ginger, minced
1 carrot, grated
1 stalk celery diced small
1 cup minced red bell pepper
1 cup minced zucchini
1/2 cup diced mushrooms
2 tsp ground corriander
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp dried rosemary
1 tsp thyme

1 cup fresh bread crumbs or rice cracker crumbs
1 cup ground nuts (almonds/pecans/cashews/walnuts)
2 cups canned brown (green) lentils
2 tbs tomato paste or tomato chili sauce
2 tbs tamari or soy sauce

salt and pepper

Cook together (covered) the brown rice, red lentils and dahl in 2 2/3 cups water - about 35 minutes until tender.

Meanwhile saute garlic and all the vegetables in olive oil until tender.  Add spices.

Preheat oven to 350.

Add canned lentils, cooked lentil rice mixture, bread crumbs. ground nuts, tomato paste, and tamari to sauteed vegetables.  Mix well.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Press into oiled loaf pan or casserole dish.   Bake uncovered for 40 minutes.

For the Gravy:

Makes 4 Cups of Cashew Gravy

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp minced fresh garlic OR 1/2 tsp powdered
  • 1 Tbsp fresh ginger, peeled and minced OR 1/2 tsp dried ginger
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Pinch cayenne powder
  • 1.4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups vegetable stock (or 1 veggie bouillon cube dissolved in 3 cups boiling water)
  • 1/3 cup cashew nut butter or 1/2 cup toasted cashews, ground fine
  • 1 Tbsp Tamari or Braggs Liquid Aminos
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Cashew Gravy Directions:

  1. In a medium sauce pan sauté the garlic and ginger in the olive oil on medium low until lightly browned
  2. Add spices and flour, and stir until bubbling
  3. Add 1 cup of stock to the ground cashews and blend until creamy
  4. Add the rest of the stock to the pot, mix, then transfer all to the blender, and whiz until smooth
  5. Pour the sauce back into the pot, bring to a boil while stirring constantly with a whisk
  6. Simmer, stirring until the gravy is smooth and thickened
  7. Add more vegetable stock, if needed, to thin the gravy
  8. Add salt, pepper and Tamari or Braggs to taste

Serve loaf and gravy with mashed potatoes for a sumptuous western style vegan dinner.
