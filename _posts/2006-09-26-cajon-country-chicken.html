--- layout: recipe title: Cajon Country Chicken category: poultry --- This spicy dish can be made up in no time.
2 tbs cornstarch 1 medium onion, sliced
1 tsp curry powder
1 (796 ml or 540 ml can stewed tomatoes
1/2 tsp thyme 
450 gm. chicken breasts cut into thin strips
2 tbs oil, divided
1 green pepper, slivered
  

Combine cornstarch, curry powder and thyme on waxed paper. Coat chicken srips.  Heat 1 tbs oil in large frying pan over medium heat.  Saute green pepper and onion for 3 minutes until tender.  Remove from pan.  Add remaining oil to pan; saute chicken until browned.  Return vegetables to pan with tomatoes.  Bring to a boil; simmer, uncovered for 10 to 15 minutes or until slightly thickened and chicken is tender.  Makes 4 servings.
