--- layout: recipe title: Air Cake (Oma's) category: desserts --- Separate 4 eggs; the whites in a smaller mixing bowl and the yolks in the large mixing bowl.

Add 1/2 tsp Cream of tartar to whites and beat until very stiff.  Set aside.

Add 2/3 cups cold water to yolks.  Beat for 5 minutes.

Add 1 1/4 cups white sugar.  Beat for 4 minutes.

Add 1 1/2 cups flour and 1 tsp vanilla.  Mix lightly. 

Gently fold in the beaten egg whites.

Bake for one hour at 325 degrees.  Remove from oven and turn pan upside down.  Cooll.   To remove from pan, separate cake from side of pan with a butter knife.

Icing

Prepare one package of cooked pudding mix reducing milk by one cup.  Let cool.
Whip 250 ml. cream.  Fold together with pudding.

Cut the cake into 2 or 3 layers.  Put either jam, fruit slices and/or some of the icing between the layers.  Cover the cake with remaining icing.  Or be creative and come up with other decorating or filling ideas.
