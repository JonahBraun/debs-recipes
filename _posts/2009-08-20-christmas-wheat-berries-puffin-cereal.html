--- layout: recipe title: Christmas Wheat Berries (Puffin Cereal) category: misc ---

1 1/2 c wheat

3 c water

2 tbs poppy seeds

 

Soak wheat during the day, the day before you need it.  In the evening add poppy seed.  Bring to boil.  Place in thermos overnight.  In the morning add honey to taste and heat.  Serve with cream.
