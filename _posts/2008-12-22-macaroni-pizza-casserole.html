--- layout: recipe title: Macaroni Pizza Casserole category: beef ---
250 g macaroni
1 tsp salt
250 g lean ground beef
1 tsp oregano leaves
2 eggs, beaten
1 onion chopped
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 green pepper, chopped
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 can tomato sauce
mozzarella cheese
1 small tomato, sliced
Cook macaroni.  Brown meat with onion, garlic, 1/2 of green pepper, 1 1/2 cups mushrooms.  Stir in tomato sauce, salt and oregano.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Combine eggs, parmesan cheese and macaroni.  Cover bottom of greased 13 X 9 inch baking dish with macaroni mixture.  Spoon meat mixutre evenly over macaroni.  Top with mozzarella slices plus remaining green pepper and mushrooms, tomato and olives.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
