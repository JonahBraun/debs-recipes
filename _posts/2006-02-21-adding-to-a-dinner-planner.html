--- layout: recipe title: Adding to a Dinner Planner category: alt --- Now that you have a 3 week dinner planner established you will want to add to it until you have at least 5 weeks on dinners on your list.

The best way to do this is to try a new recipe once a week or every couple of weeks.  If you like it add it to the list.

You can get new dinner ideas from magazines, newspapers, friends, cook books or on the web.

It is important that new additions are not too difficult to make or require hard to find ingredients.  Don't put a dinner on the planner until you have tried it.  You may not like it or it may be too cumbersome to make.
