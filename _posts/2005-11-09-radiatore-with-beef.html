--- layout: recipe title: Radiatore with Stir-fry Beef category: beef --- Quick and easy!
150 gm  pkg of radiatore 1 carrot, cut into julienne strips
2 tbs oil
1 onion, chopped
500 gm  beef steak, cubed
4 cloves garlic, minced
 cup beef broth, divided
1 tbs corn starch
2 cups broccoli florets
 1/4 cup each waterr and ketchup
2 cups fresh mushrooms, quartered
 1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce

If time allows marinate beef in a bit of barbecue sauce and soy sauce.  Cook radiatore.  In a wok or skillet, heat oil; stir fry beef until browned.  Remove beef from wok; keep warm.  Add 1/4 cup broth to wok, stirring and scraping browned bits from bottom.  Stir in broccoli, mushrooms, carrot, onion and garlic.  Cover and steam until vegetables are tender-crisp.  Mix remaining broth and cornstarch.; add waerr, ketchup and hot pepper sauce and stir mixutre into vegetables.  Cook stirring constantly, untl bubbly and thickened.  Return beef t wok and heat through.  Toss with radiatore.
