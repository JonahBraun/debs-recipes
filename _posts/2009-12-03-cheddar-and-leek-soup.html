--- layout: recipe title: Cheddar and Leek Soup category: soups ---

1/4 c butter

1 bunch leeks (white and light green parts only), chopped

1 onion, chopped

1/3 c flour

3 c chicken broth

2 c water

1 can English style ale

1/4 tsp salt

1/2 c whipping cream

12 oz (about 4 cups ) aged (3-5 year) Cheddar cheese shredded 

2 tsp hot mustard

1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

1/4 tsp pepper

 

In large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; cook leeks and onion, stiring often, until softened, 5 minutes.

Add flour cook, stirring, just until starting to brown, about 2 minutes.  Slowly stir in broth, water, ale and salt; bring to simmer.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Stir in cream; bring just to boil over medium heat.  Add cheese, mustard, Worcestershire sauce and pepper; reduce heat to low.  Stir until poping hot and cheese is melted. 

Makes 8 serverings.
