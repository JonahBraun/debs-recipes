--- layout: recipe title: Curry Rice category: sides --- This rice is really really good!  Just like what you get in the Asian restaurants.
2 c cooked rice                        salt and pepper
½ tbs. curry powder                ¼ c butter
½ tsp. garlic powder                ½ c minced onions
½ tsp. paprika


Combine onions and butter in sauce pan.  Heat until onions are clear.  Add spices and rice, cook for a few minutes.
