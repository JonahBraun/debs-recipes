--- layout: recipe title: Fruit Crisp category: desserts --- This crisp works well with a variety of fresh or fresh and frozen fruit combined.  It has a nice spicy flavor.
1//2 cup sugar 1/3 cup butter
2 tbs cornstarch
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/8 tsp cloves
1/2 cup flour
dash cardamon
1/2 cup oats
4 cups sliced fruit
1 1/2 tsp grated lemon peel
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 tbs cinnamon sugar
  

For fruit mixture:  Combine sugar, cornstarch, cloves, cardamon, then mix with sliced fruit.  Place fruit mixture into a 8 1/2 X 11 inch buttered baking dish.

For crumble:  Combine butter, sugar, flour, rolled oats, nutmeg and lemon peel.  Mix to crumbly consistency.  Place on top of fruit.  Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40 -45 minutes.
