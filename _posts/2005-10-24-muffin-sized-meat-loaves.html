--- layout: recipe title: Muffin-Sized Pizza Meat Loaves category: beef --- The best mini meat loaf yet!
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 cup finely shredded carrot
1/4 cup fine bread crumbs
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 egg, beaten
1/3 cup finely shredded onion
1 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp pepper

Combine all ingredients except 1/4 cup of the sauce.  Mix lightly to blend.  Press mxture into 12 muffin cups.  Top each with remaining sauce.  Bake at 375 for 25 to 30 min.
