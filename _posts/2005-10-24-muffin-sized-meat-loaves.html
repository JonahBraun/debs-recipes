--- layout: recipe title: Muffin-Sized Pizza Meat Loaves category: beef --- The best mini meat loaf yet! - 1½ lb ground beef - ½ c finely shredded carrot - ¼ c fine bread crumbs - ½ t salt - ½ cup tomato sauce - 1 egg, beaten - ⅓ c finely shredded onion - 1 t dried oregano - ¼ t pepper Combine all ingredients except 1/4 c of the sauce. Mix lightly to blend. Press mxture into 12 muffin cups. Top each with remaining sauce. Bake at 375 for 25 to 30 minutes.
