Deb grew up on the Canadian prairies on a steady diet of rather bland European foods.  When she hit the big city as a young adult her insatiable curiosity and desire for change prompted a rash of experimentation with cooking and diet.  As a result, husband Ed and their four growing children thrived on a vegetarian diet for about a decade before settling into their present diet of mostly natural and organic foods of all kinds.

Good cooking is catchy, so as the kids grew older they often contribute (along with their spouses) to the family recipe collection.
