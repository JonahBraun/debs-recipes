--- layout: recipe title: The Best Granola Recipe category: misc --- Less sugar, less fat.... and great taste!
2 kg. regular oat flakes
1 kg. combination of 9 grain, porridge oats, etc.(I use whatever flakes I can get my hands on).
1/2 cup seseme seeds (optional)
1/2 cup sunflower seeds (optional)
2 1/2 cups coarsly chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pecans)
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tbs. vanilla
1 1/2  cups brown sugar
1 1/2 cups coconut flakes
1 3/4 cups oil
2  cups chopped dried fruit


Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.  Spread mixture in a broiler pan to about 1/2 inch thick.  Bake at 350 for 8 - 10 minutes or until slightly browned.  Cool in a large pan, adding chopped dried fruit.  Store up to 2 months in airtight containers.
