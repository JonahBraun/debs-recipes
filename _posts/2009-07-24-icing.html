Butter Cream Icing

1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup butter
4 cups icing sugar
1 tsp. flavoring

 Cream shortening and butter.  Add icing sugar 1/2 cup at a time beating well after each addition.  Add flavoring and paste food color is desired.

 

Cooked Butter Cream Icing

1 cup white sugar
1 cup unsalted butter
3 tbs cornstarch
 1 tsp flavoring
1 cup milk
 

 Mix sugar and cornstarch until smooth.  Slowly add milk while stirring.  Cook over medium heat until thickened.  Remove from heat and cover with wax paper.  Cool completely.  Whip the butter until creamy.  Add the pudding mixture and flavoring and beat until light and fluffy.  Add paste food color if desired.
