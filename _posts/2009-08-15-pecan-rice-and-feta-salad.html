--- layout: recipe title: Pecan, Rice and Feta Salad category: sides ---

1 - 7 oz package wild pecan long grain rice

1 c feta cheese crumbled

1/2 c green pepper chopped

1/2 c yellow pepper chopped

1/2 c onion chopped

1/2 c toasted pecans chopped

2 oz pimento chopped

 

Dressing:

1/4 c olive oil

2 tbs tarragon white wine vinegar

freshly ground black pepper

 

Prepare rice according to package directions, using 1 tsp salt.  Cool.

 

Prepare dressing by combining in a small bowl and whisking together

 

Add the rice, feta, peppers, onions and pimento to a bowl.  Add the dressing and toss to blend.  Chill for up to 24 hours.
