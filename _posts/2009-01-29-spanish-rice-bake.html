--- layout: recipe title: Spanish Rice Bake category: sides ---
1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 cup chili Sauce
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
3/4 cup uncooked long grain rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tsp salt
1 - 14.5 oz can tomatoes1 tsp brown sugar
1 cup water
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp worchestershire sauce1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
pinch ground black pepper
chopped cilantro

Brown ground beef in a large skillet.  Transfer to oven proof casserole. Stir in all remaining ingredients except cilantro. Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese and bake for additional 10 to 15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.  Garnish with fresh cilantro.
