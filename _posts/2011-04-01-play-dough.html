Play Dough

 Recipe #1

1/2 c salt

2 tbs cooking oil

2 c flour

1 tbs alum

2 c boiling water

food coloring

 

Mix salt and cooking oil together.  Add boiling water and food coloring.  Mix in flour and alum

 

Recipe #2

1/2 c salt

1 tbs oil

1 c flour

2 tbs creme of tartar 

1 c boiling water

food coloring

 

Mix salt and cooking oil together.  Add boiling water and food coloring.  Mix in flour and creme of tartar.
