--- layout: recipe title: Falafel category: vegi ---
1 lb garbanzp beans
2 slices bread
2 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup chopped parsely
4 garlic cloves, peeled
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup cracked wheat
1 tsp coriander
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp black pepper
oil for frying
2 tbs bread crumbs

 

  
Soak beans overnight in 10 cups of water and the baking soda.  Drain and grind twice in an electric grinder.  Set i a large mixing bowl.  Wet the bread under running water then squeeze and grind with the garlic and parsley.  Add to the ground beans.  Soak the cracked wheat for 20 minutes in hot water and drain.  Add the wheat, seasonings and bread crumbs.  Mix well. Let stand for 115 minutes.  Form 1 inch balls.  Deep fry in 3 inches of oil.  Fry the balls until golden brown on both sides.
