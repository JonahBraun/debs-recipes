--- layout: recipe title: Whole Wheat Bannock category: breads ---


1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour 1/2 cup white flour 
 1 tbs baking powder 1/2 tsp salt
 1/4 cup shortening 3/4 cup milk (scant)
1/2 - 1 cup fresh or dried berries    

 Mix dry ingredients together.  Blend in shortening.  Stir in enough milk to make a soft but not sticky dough.  Add fruit if desired.  Knead until smooth.  Pat down into well greased cast iron fry pan and fry until golden, then flip.  Or place pan in 400 degree oven and bake for about 12 minutes.
