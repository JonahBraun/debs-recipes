--- layout: recipe title: Ginger Snaps category: desserts --- By far the tastiest ginger snaps ever, I found this recipe while living in Inuvik.
2 c flour                        1 c sugar
½ tsp. salt                     ¾ c *butter
1 tbs. ginger                 1 egg
1 tsp. cinnamon            ¼ c black strap molasses
2 tsp. soda


Mix dry ingredients.  In separate bowl blend butter, sugar, egg and molasses.  Mix dry and wet ingredients together.  Make into tsp. size balls.  Roll in white sugar.  Place on greased cookie sheet and press down with fork.  Bake at 375F for 10-15 minutes.

* Never substitute margarine or shortening for butter.  Butter gives the best flavor.
