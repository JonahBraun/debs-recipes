The best banana bread I've come across yet! Very moist!

- 1½ c flour
- 1 egg
- 1½ t baking powder
- 1 c mashed bananas
- ¼ t baking soda
- ¾ c sugar
- ¼ t cinnamon
- ¼ c cooking oil

Mix alltogether. Pour batter into greased loaf pan. 
Bake at 350 F for 50-55 minutes.
