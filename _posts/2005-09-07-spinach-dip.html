--- layout: recipe title: Spinach Dip category: starters --- A basic spinach dip recipe that's great tasting and easy to make.
2 c fresh chopped spinich            1 c mayo
1 pkg. Veggie soup mix                8 oz. sour cream



Slightly cook spinich.  Mix all ingredients.  Chill.  When ready to serve put into bread bowl.


NOTE: The key to this recipe is to use fresh spinach.
