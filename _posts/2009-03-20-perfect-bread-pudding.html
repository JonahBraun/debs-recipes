This is a very easy and satisfying bread pudding recipe that can be made with what you have in your fridge! Tastes good with caramel/butterscotch sauce on top.

3/4 loaf of bread, buttered and toasted
2 cups milk
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbs vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup raisins or chopped nuts (optional)

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease with butter 3-quart casserole dish.

Combine sugar, eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon. Mix well.

Break up toasted bread, and put in casserole dish. Add raisins or nuts. Pour egg mixture over bread, and stir.

Bake 30 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
