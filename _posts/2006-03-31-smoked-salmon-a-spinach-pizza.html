--- layout: recipe title: Smoked Salmon & Spinach Pizza category: seafood --- This recipe is from Ryan and Amanda.  It is absolutely deliciious!
 1 extra large or 2 medium pizza crusts
creamy caesar dressing
2 cups spinach leaves
300 gms smoked salmon
2/3 cup walnut halves
 parmesan cheese
thinly sliced green or white onion
 capers


Partially bake pizza crust.  Spread with a thin layer of caesar dressing.  Top with spinach leave, sliced smoked salmon, walnut halves, parmesan cheese, onions and capers.  Bake at 400 degreees for approximately 10 minutes.  Remove from oven and add additional cheese and small blobs of caesar dressing.

Serve hot.
