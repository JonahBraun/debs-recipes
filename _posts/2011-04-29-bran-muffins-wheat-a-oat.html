--- layout: recipe title: Bran Muffins - Wheat & Oat category: breads ---
 1 cup wheat bran
1/3 cup brown sugar
 1/2 cup oat bran 1/2 tsp vanilla
 1 cup milk
 2 tbs blackstrap molasses
 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
 1/2 cup white unbleached flour
 1 egg
 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
 1 tsp baking soda
 1/2 tsp salt
 1 tsp baking powder

 1 cup raisins

 Mix all ingredients together. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Bake in greased muffin tin or use paper liners.  Makes 12 medium sized muffins.
