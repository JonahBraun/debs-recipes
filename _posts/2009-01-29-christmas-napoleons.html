1/3 pkg phyllo dough, thawed
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tbs sugar
 

 Filling

1/3 cup water
1/3 cup dried cranberries
1 1/2 cups whipping cream, divided
1 tsp grated orange peel

 Cranberry Coulis

2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
1/2 cup sugar
2/3 cups water
2 oranges in segments

Preheat oven to 350.

Slice phyllo into 1/2" ribbons.  Gently toss phyllo to separate ribbon layers.  

Melt butter and sugar until sugar dissolves.  Drizzle 1 tbs of mixture over phyllo and toss.

Divide phyllo evenly onto 2 buttered cookie sheets making 18 loose piles.  Shape each pile into 3 1/2 inch round nests. Drizzle with remaining butter mixture.  Bake 10 minutes until golden and crisp.   Cool.

Filling:

In a small saucepan, bring water and dried cranberries to a boil.  Cool and drain.  Meanwhile melt chocolate with 1/4 cup cream.  Stir in orange peel.   Cool.

Beat remaining cream to soft peaks and gently fold chocolate into whipped cream.  Stir in dried cranberries. Cover and chill.

Cranberry Coulis:

In small saucepan bring fresh cranberries, sugar and water o boil.  Cover and simmer 10 min.  Strain through sieve.  Cool.

To serve:

Spoon coulis onto dessert plate.  Top with phyllo nests and a spoonful of chcolate filling.  Repeat layers.  Top with third phyllo nests..  Garnish with oranges.
