--- layout: recipe title: Simone's Pork Spice Rub category: pork ---

This spice rub is good on pork roast, pork chops, chicken and even lamb.Â  It makes a large quantity that can be stored in an air tight container.

 

1/4 c white sugar

1/4 c brown sugar

1/8 c seasoned salt

1/8 c onion powder

1/8 c garlic powder

1/8 c celery salt

1/4 c paprika

1 tbs ground black pepper

1 tbs chili powder

1 tsp mustard powder

1/2 tsp poultry seasoning

1/2 tsp ground ginger

1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

1/4 tsp allspice

 

Combine all ingredients together and store in airtight container in cool dark place.
