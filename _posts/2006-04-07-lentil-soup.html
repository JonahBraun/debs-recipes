--- layout: recipe title: Lentil Soup category: soups --- Hearty and spicy!  For best results simmer on very low heat all day and serve in the evening.
1/2  onion, chopped  2 cloves garlic, minced
 1 stalk celery, diced 1 carrot, sliced
 other chopped vegis 1 tin tomato paste
 1 cup lentils, brown, green or red 1 packet of beef broth or 2 beef sausages or 1/2 lb. ground beef
 olive oil tsp. fresh grated ginger
 1 tsp. garam masala (opt)

 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

 1 tsp ground cardamon

 1 tsp. cumin

 salt & pepper


Saute onion, celery, garlic and ginger along with sausage,  in oil.  Add 4-5 cups of water and the lentils.  Bring to a boil.  Add tomato paste, beef boullion, carrots and other vegetables.  Add spices and simmer on very low heat for at least 2 hours.  Serve hot.
