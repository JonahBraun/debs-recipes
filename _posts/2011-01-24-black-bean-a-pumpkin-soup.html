--- layout: recipe title: Black Bean & Pumpkin Soup category: soups ---

Very easy and filling.  Great for a hot meal on a cold busy day.

Serves 8 - 10.  Great Served with focaccia or quesadillas

 

3, 15 oz. cans black beans, rinsed and drained                     1, 16 oz can solid pack pumpkin

1, 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes, drained                               1/2 c dry sherry

1 yellow onion, chopped                                                    3 tbs sherry vinegar (or red wine vinegar)

2 large shallots, chopped                                                   salt to taste

4 closed garlic (or more), crushed                                      sour cream for garnish

1 1/2 tsp ground cumin                                                    toasted pumpkin seeds for garnish

4 c beef (or vegetable) broth

 

Place the beans, tomatoes, onion, shallots, garlic, cumin, broth, pumpkin, and sherry in the slow cooker.  Stir to combine.  Cover and cook on low for 6 - 7 hours.

Puree the soup in the cooker with a handheld immersion blender or in batches in a food processor or blender.  Add the vinegar and stir to combine.  Add salt if necessary.   Serve with a dollop of sour cream and toasted pumpkin seeds sprinkled on top.
