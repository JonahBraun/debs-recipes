Marinade:

1 1-pound beef tenderloin roast,                        1/4 c Asian sesame oil
   cut into 1/2 in. thick slices                               1/4 c chopped peeled fresh ginger
9 garlic cloves, chopped                                    1/4 c chopped green onions 
1/4 c hoisin sauce                                              2 tbs. sesame seeds
1/4 c hot chili sauce or hot chili paste                1 tbs. fresh lime juice
1/4 c mirin (sweet Japanese rice wine
   found at T&T)


Dressing:

3 tbs. fresh lime juice                                          1 1/2 tsp. finely grated peeled fresh ginger
2 tbs. soy souce                                                 1/2 tsp. Asian sesame oil
2 tbs. honey                                                        1/2 c olive oil
1 tbs. rice vinegar                                               1/4 c chopped fresh cilantro
1 1/2 tsp. mirin


Salad:

1 6-oz. package soba noodles (found at           18 small fresh basil leaves
   Superstore)                                                     18 fesh cilantro leaves
2 c chopped grean onions                                 18 fresh mint leaves
1 c matchstick-size strips peeled carrot     
1 c matchstick-size strips daikon


For Marinade:

Place beef in large resealable plastic bag.  Whisk garlic, hoisin sauce, hot chili sauce, mirin, seame oil, ginger, green onoins, seamce seeds, and lime juice in medium bowl to blend; pour over beef and seal bag.  Turn bag over to coat beef.  Refrigerate overnight, turning bag occasionally.


For Dressing:

Combine first 7 ingredients in blender.  With blender running, gradually add olive oil.  Add chopped cilantro; blend until smooth.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Dressing can be made 2 hours ahead.  Cover and chill.


For Salad:

Cook soba noodles in large soucepan of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bite, stirring occasionaly.  Drain; rinse under cold water.  Drainwill.  Transfer noodles to large bowl; drizzle with 2 tablespoons dressing.  Let stand at room temperature.

Prepare barbeque (medium high heat).  Remove beef from marinade; discard marinade.  Grill beef until cooked to desired doneness, about 2 minutes per sidefor medium rare.  Let stand at room temperature 5 minutes.  Cut beef against grain tiunto thin strips.  Add to noodles.

Add green onions and all remaining ingredients to bowl with noodles and beef.  Add remaining dressing and toss to coat.  Divide among 6 bowls and serve.
