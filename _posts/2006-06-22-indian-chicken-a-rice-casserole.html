--- layout: recipe title: Indian Chicken & Rice Casserole category: poultry ---
1/4 cup flaked coconut 1 tbs Pataks Curry Paste
1/2 onion, minced
3 tsp oil
500 gm chicken breask, cubed
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper or more to taste
2 cups cauliflower flowerets
1 cup frozen peas and carrots (or fresh)
1 398 ml canned diced tomatoes
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup raisons
toasted sliced almonds and flaked coconut for garnish
  
 1 cup basmati rice
2 cups coconut milk 
  
  
Cook rice in  milk and set aside.

Saute onion, cauliflower, and carrots in olive oil.  Set aside.

Mix curry paste, coconut, and cayenne pepper.  Add this to chicken and place in hot oiled pan. Fry until chicken is done.  Remove from pan and add to sauteed vegetables.
Add peas,  canned tomatoes with their juice and the raisons.  Transfer this mixture to the chicken and vegetable bowl.  Stir in yogurt until wel mixed.

Serve togeher with cooked rice garnished with coconut and almonds.
