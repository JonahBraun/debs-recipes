--- layout: recipe title: Thai Pizza category: poultry --- This recipe comes from Company's Company Asian Cooking. We have adapted it to fit our family's taste and cooking style. 1 homemade or commercial pizza crust - peanut sauce - commercial or recipe below: - 2 tbs chopped cilantro - 2 cloves garlic - 1 1/2 tsp chili paste (Sambal oelek) - 1 tsp sesame oil - 1/3 cup peanut butter - 3 tbs. golden corn syrup - 2 tbs. rice vinegar - 1/2 cup coconut milk - 2 tbs. lime juice Grind first 4 ingredients together.  Heat oil and add paste.  Cook just until fragrant.  Add peanut butter, syrup, vinegar, coconut milk and lime juice.  Heat and stir until starts to bubble.  Cool to room temperature.

Pizza:

3/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese
1/2 chicken breast cut into thin slices
1 medium carrot, cut into julienne strips
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbs oil
1 large red pepper cut into rings
1 cup fresh bean sprouts
3 green onions, sliced diagonally
1 tsp toasted sesame seeds.

To assemble:

Spread peanut sauce on crust.  Top with grated cheese.

Saute chicken, carrot and cayenne pepper in oil.  Arrange on top of cheese.  Top with red peppers.
Bake at 425 for 15 minutes.  Remove from oven and sprinkle with bean sprouts, green onion and sesame seeds.

Serve.
