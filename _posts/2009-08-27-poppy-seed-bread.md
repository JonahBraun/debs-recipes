Poppy Seed Filling

- 2 c poppy seeds
- 1 c milk
- 1/2 c honey
- 1/4 c sugar
- 1/8 t salt
- 2 eggs
  
Combine seeds, milk, honey, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat till thick. Let cool, then beat in eggs. Use this poppy seed mixture with sweet bread dough (mixed in the bread maker). </p>
