--- layout: recipe title: Autumn GLazed Apple Cream Pie category: desserts ---

1 can condensed milk

1 1/2 c sour cream

1/4 c frozen apple juice concentrate, thawed

1 egg

1 tsp vanilla

1 graham crust

2 apples, peeled and sliced

2 tbs butter

1/2 c apricot preserves, warmed

 

Whisk together conensed milk, sour cream, juice concentrate, egg and vanilla.  Pour into pie crust.  Bake in preheated 350 F oven for 25 - 30 minutes or until center is set.  Cool.

Saute apples in butter until tender, arrange on top of baked pie.  Spoon preserves over apples to glaze.  Chill thoroughly.
