--- layout: recipe title: Raise the Roof Chili Chicken category: poultry ---
4 tbs olive oil
3 chicken breasts cut into strips
1 red onion coarsely chopped
1/2 red pepper, cut into thin strips
1 garlic clove, minced
1 small head broccoli, cut into bite sized pieces
2 tsp chili powder
1 tbs worcestershire sauce
2 tbs chipped cilantro
1/2 - 1 tbs Louisiana hot sauce
1 cup grated mozzarella or cheddar cheese 
salt and pepper to taste
  
Under broiler, brown chicken and vegis. Add spices and combine until evenly coated.  Sprinkle with cheese and eturn to broiler until cheese melts and is lightly brown.  Serve immediately spooned over Cheese and Jalapeno Corn Flap Jacks.
 
 Cheese & Jalapeno Corn Flap Jacks
 
 1 1/3 cups corn meal  2 cups buttermilk (or 2/3 cup plain yogurt and 1 1/3 cups milk
 1 1/4 tsp salt
 1 jalapeno pepper seeded and chopped
 1/2 tsp baking soda 1/2 cup grated cheddar
 1/4 cup flour
 2 eggs
 1/4 melted butter
 
 Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.  Beat eggs and liquids in another bowl; add cheese and jalapeno.  Stir liquid ingredients into dry and stir only until combined.  Fry on hot griddle.
