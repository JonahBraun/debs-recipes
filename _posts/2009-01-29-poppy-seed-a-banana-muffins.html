--- layout: recipe title: Poppy Seed & Banana Muffins category: breads ---
1/2 cup milk
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
2 tsp lemon juice
1 cup bran
1/3 cup poppy seeds
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/4 cup oil
1 tsp soda
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
2 medium bananas
 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 

Combine milk, lemon juice and poppy seeds and set aside.  Beat together oil, honey and eggs.  Stir in mashed bananas.   Combine with poppy seed mixture.  Stir dry ingredients together in a large bowl.  Pour in liquid mixture.  Stir until just blended.  Bake 20 - 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
