--- layout: recipe title: Caesar Salad Dressing category: salads --- Really nice and creamy!
1/4 tsp salt 1 egg
1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp lemon juice
 1/2 tsp parmesan cheese
2 cloves crushed garlic
 2 tsp wine vinegar
2 drops Worchestershire  sauce            
1/3 cup olive oil



Blend all ingredients except olive oil.  Then with blender running slowly add the olive oil and blend until thick and creamy.
