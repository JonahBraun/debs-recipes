--- layout: recipe title: Strawberry-Rhubarb Crisp category: desserts ---
1/2 cup sugar
2 tbs corn strarch
1/8 tsp cloves
dash cardamom

1 10-oz pkg frozen strawberries, thawed
3 cups diced rhubarb

1/3 cup soft butter
2/3 cup brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup quick-cooking rolled oats
1 1/2 tsp lemon peel
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 tbs cinnamon sugar

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Combine sugar, arrowroot, cloves & cardamom.

Add strawberries & rhubarb.

Mix well.

Pour into buttered 1 1/2 quart baking dish.

Mix remaining ingredients with pastry blender or fork until crumbly.

Sprinkle over strawberries & rhubarb.

Bake in 350F oven for 40-45 mins.

Serves 6.
