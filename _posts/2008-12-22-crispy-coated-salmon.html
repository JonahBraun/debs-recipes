--- layout: recipe title: Crispy-Coated Salmon category: seafood ---
4 salmon steaks
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 tbs finely chopped parsley
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tbs butter
1 egg beaten
2 tbs oil
Combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese and seasonings.  Place seasoned crumbs on a large flat plate.  Pour beaten egg onto a secnd plate.  Dip salmon in beaten egg and then in seasoned crumbs, pressing firmly.  Regrigerate coated steaks 1/2 hour to help coating adhere.  Heat frying pan and add butter and oil.  When butter melts, fry steaks for 10 min. per inch of thickness, turning once halfway immediately with Honey Lime Sauce, if desired.
 
 Honey Lemon Sauce
 1/2 cup mayonnaise
 2 tsp. honey
 1/4 cup yogurt
 1/4 tsp curry powder
2 tbs lime juice
 
  
 Hot Cucumber Sauce
 
 1/2 cup sour cream
 1/2 tsp dill weed
 1/2 cup chopped cucumber
 2 tsp chopped green onion
 1/2 tsp salt
 pinch white pepper
