--- layout: recipe title: Chicken Breasts Diane category: poultry --- Easy to make, but delicious and elegant.
 4 large boneles chicken breast halves
3 tbs brandy or cognac
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbs chopped parsley
1/4 to 1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tsp dijon style mustard
2 tbs olive oil
1/4 cup chicken broth
2 tbs butter
 3 tbs chopped fresh chives
juice of 1/2 lime or lemon
 or green onion



Place chicken breast halves between sheets of waxed paper.   Pound slightly with mallet.  Sprinkle with salt and black pepper.  Heat 1 tbs each of oil and butterr in large skillet.  Cook chicken over high heat for 4 minutes on each side.  Do not over cook or they will become dry.  Transfer to warm serving platter.

Add chives or green onion, lime juiice, brandy, parsley and mustard to pan.  Cook 5 seconds, whisking constantly. Add broth; stir until smooth.  Whisk in remaining butter and oil.  Pour sauce over chicken.  Serve immediately.
