--- layout: recipe title: Cheddar Cheese Muffins category: breads ---

2 c flour

1/4 c sugar

1 tbs baking powder

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp soda

1 1/2 c grated cheddar cheese

1 c plain yogert

1/4 c melted butter

2 eggs, beaten

 

Sift dry ingredients and cheddar cheese. Combine remaining ingredients and mix into the flour mixture.  Stir until moistened.  Dallop on greased cook sheet  Bake at 350 F for 10-15 minutes.  If desired, when muffins come out of the oven brush with the following mixture:

melted butter

parsley

garlic powder
