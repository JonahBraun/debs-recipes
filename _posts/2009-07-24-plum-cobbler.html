--- layout: recipe title: Plum Cobbler category: desserts ---

4 cups quartered pitted prune plums
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbs quick cooking tapioca
3 tbs shortening
1/3 cup orange juice
2 tbs coconut
1 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp grated orange rind
3 tsp baking powder2/3 cup milk
pouring cream (optional) 

 Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Butter a 12 X 7 baking dish, and add plums.  Mix 1/4 cup sugar, tapioca and orange juice and drizzle over fruit.  Sift next three ingredients and 2 tbs sugar into a medium bowl.  Add shortening and cut in finely.  Stir in coconut and orange rind with a fork.  Add milk and mix lightly with fork to make a soft, puffy dough.

Knead gently.  Roll or pat into an oblong slightly smaller than baking dish and cut into 6 squares.  Lay squares over fruit, cut a small slash in centre of each and sprinkle lightly with sugar.  Bake until topping is nicely browned and fruit is bubbling, about 25 minutes.
