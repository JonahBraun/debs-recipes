--- layout: recipe title: Poppy Seed (for Popy seed bread) category: desserts ---

2 c poppy seeds

1 c milk

1/2 c honey

1/4 c sugar

1/8 tsp salt

2 eggs

 

Combine seeds, milk, honey, sugar, and salt.  Cook over medium heat till thick.  Let cool, beat in eggs, nuts or raisins if desired.

 

Use this poppy seed mixture with sweet bread dough (mixed in the bread maker).
