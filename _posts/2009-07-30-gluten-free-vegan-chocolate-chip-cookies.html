--- layout: recipe title: Gluten Free, Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies category: desserts --- These gluten free cookies taste great and also have regular cookie consistency. - ½ c blanched almond flour (not Red Mill; it doesn't have the right consistency) - ½ t sea salt - 2 t baking soda - ½ c grapeseed oil - 1 T vanilla - ⅓ c honey, melted - 1 c dark chocolate Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl. Stir together wet ingredients and a smaller bowl. Mix wet ingredients into dry. Form 1/2 inch balls, flatten and place on well greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 F for 7 - 10 minutes.
