--- layout: recipe title: Shells with Parsley-Basil Pesto category: sides ---
1 cup parsley leaves
 1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
 1/2 cup basil leaves
1/3 cup olive oil
 5 cloves garlic
1/4 cup slivered almonds
 2 tbs lemon juice
250 g large shells
 
In blender, combine parsley, basil, garlic, almonds, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  Process just until combined.  Slowly add oil, then 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.  Process until well blended.  Cook shells.  Toss shells with pesto and serve topped with remaining cheese.
