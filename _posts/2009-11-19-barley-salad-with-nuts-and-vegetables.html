--- layout: recipe title: Barley Salad with Nuts and Vegetables category: salads ---
1 cup barley
 2 celery sticks, diced
2 medium carrots, diced  1 apple diced
1/2 cup pecans toasted and chopped
 1/3 cup fresh dill, chopped
1/3 cup feta cheese, crumbled
 2 tbs lemon juice
3 tbs. olive oil
 salt and pepper to taste

 Put barley into saucepan with 2 cups water and 1/2 tsp salt.  Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 15 - 20 minutes until done.  Remove from heat cover and let sit for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.

Place barley in bowl and add all other ingredients.  Mix well.   Salt and pepper to taste.  Refrigerate until serving.
