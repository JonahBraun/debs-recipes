--- layout: recipe title: Chickpea Picadillo category: vegi --- Great Vegan Dish!
2 - 15 oz cans chickpeas 1 - 14 oz can diced tomatoes
14 oz pcg LightLife Gimme Lean or Vegi Ground Round
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 med. onion, chopped
1 inch piece ginger, grated
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp cloves
1 tsp cumin
 1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp cardamon
 1/2 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp salt
 2 bay leaves
1 inch piece cinnamon stick
Combine all ingredients in a casserole dish.  Bring to a boil in your microwave, then place in low oven (225 - 250 degrees) for several hours so that all the flavors absorb into the beans.  Serve on a bed of rice.
