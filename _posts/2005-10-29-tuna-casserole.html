--- layout: recipe title: Hearty Tuna Casserole category: seafood ---
2 cans tuna or salmon  1/2 cup mayonaisse
3 cups uncooked noodles
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 cup chopped celery
 1/4 tsp salt
1/3 sliced green onions
 1 cup shredded monterey jack or other cheese
1/2 - 2/3 cup sour cream
 1 chopped tomato
2 tsp prepared mustard
 1 small zucchini, sliced

Cook noodles.  Combine with all other ingredients except zucchini, tomatoes and cheese .  Place in casserole dish.  Top with zucchini and cheese.  Bake at 350 for 30 min.   Garnish with diced tomatoes.
