--- layout: recipe title: Mandarin Trifle category: desserts --- Egg Nog Custard:

- 2 -4 oz packages - 3 c eggnog

- Pour eggnog into a medium bowl, add pudding mix. Beat with whisk or mixer at lowest speed for about 2 minutes. Let sit 5 minutes.

2 c whipping cream
1/3 c icing sugar

4 c 1 inch cubes of sponge cake or Oma's Air Cake

4 tbs orange juice or orange liqueur (Triple Sec), divided

2 -10 oz cans mandarin orange segments, drained

toasted slice almonds

Beat whipping cream with icing sugar until stiff peaks form.  Arrange half the sponge cake in the bottom of a 3 quart glass bowl.  Sprinkle with 2 tbs orange juice.  Cover with half the custard, then half the mandarin oranges (reserve a few for garnishing), followed by half of the whipping cream. Repeat the layers.  The last layer of whipping cream may be piped through a pastry tube if desired.  Garnish with almonds and mandarin oranges.  Chill several hours before serving.  Serves 10 - 12.
