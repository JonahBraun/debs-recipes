--- layout: recipe title: Dark Fruit Cake category: desserts ---

1 lb raisins

1 lb pitted dated

1 lb citron

1 lb currants

1 1/2 lbs candied cherries

1/2 lb candied orange peel

1/4 lb candied lemon peel

1 1/2 c chopped nuts

1 1/2 lb butter

1 lb light brown sugar

12 eggs beaten

4 c sifted flour

1 tsp soda

1/2 tsp nutmeg

1/2 tsp mace

1/2 tsp cloves

1 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp allspice

1 tsp salt

1 c molasses

 

Combine fruit and nuts. Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, blend.  Sift dry ingredients and add creamed mixture with molasses.  Add butter to fruits and nuts.  Mix thoroughly using hands if necessary.  Pour into pans which have been greased and prepared.  Bake at 275 C for about 4 hours 20 minutes.  Remove foil last 30 minutes to allow tops to dry.
