--- layout: recipe title: Pumpkin Cheesecake category: desserts --- Tastes as good as it looks.

Crust:                                    

1/4 cup butter                                        1 1/4 cups ginger snaps, crushed

 
                        Filling:                                                         


2 X 250 gm cream cheese*                      2/3 cup suger
2 eggs                                                   1 3/4 cups pumpkin
1/2 tsp cinamon                                      1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp ginger                                         1/2 tsp salt
  
 * substitute 1 cup yogurt for one of the pkg. cream cheese for a lighter pie.


Mix together crust ingredients and press into pie pan.  Bake crust at 350 for about 8 min.

Blend pie ingredients together in blender.  Pour into crust and bake at 350 for about 1 hour or until knife comes out clean.  Chill.  Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate curls.
