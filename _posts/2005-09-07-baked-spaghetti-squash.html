--- layout: recipe title: Spaghetti Squash Bake category: sides --- Another great way to serve spaghetti squash.
1 spaghetti squash (about 3 lb.)              1/3 c sliced green onions
3 tbs. olive oil                                            ½ tsp. salt
1 minced clove garlic                                ¼ tsp. pepper
½ tsp. dried oregano                                1/3 c grated Parmesan cheese
½ lb. thinly sliced mushrooms


Slice squash in half, remove seeds and bake at 350F for 45 minutes to 1 hour until tender.  Combine all ingredients except squash and cheese in large skillet.  Cook until mushrooms are just tender.  Using fork, removed squash from shell and add to mushroom mixture.  Toss well.  Place in serving dish and sprinkly with Parmesan cheese.
