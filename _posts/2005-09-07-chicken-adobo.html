--- layout: recipe title: Chicken Adobo category: poultry --- Super easy to make
2 lb. chicken (or tofu)                     4 crushed garlic cloves
½ c vinegar                                     ¼ tsp. pepper
¼ c soy sauce                                2-3 pc. bay leaf
½ c water                                        1-2 tsp. brown sugar


Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan.  Cover and bring to boil.  Cooke for about 45-60 minutes or until chicken is cooked.  Uncover and simmer for about 15 minutes or until sauce is slightly thickened and chicken is lightly browned.  Serve warm with white rice.
