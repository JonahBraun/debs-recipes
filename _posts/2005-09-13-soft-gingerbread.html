--- layout: recipe title: Gingerbread category: desserts --- Soft gingerbread perfect cut into squares for a Christmas snack.
1 c molasses                  2 eggs
½ c sugar                       1 tsp. ginger
½ c butter                       1 tsp. cinnamon
½ c sour milk                 1 tsp. soda dissolved in milk
2 ½ c flour

Combine all ingredients.  Bake at 350 F in a greased 9 X 9 pan for 30 minutes.
