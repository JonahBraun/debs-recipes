--- layout: recipe title: Gabrielle's Yorkshire pudding category: breads --- This is cousin Gabrielle's yorkshire pudding recipe; the best I've had so far.

Gabrielle says, "Usually when I take my roast out of the oven is when I put mine in, it gives the roast a nice time to cool for cutting and both are ready at the same time".
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbs water
 

Mix all ingredients (except water) early in the day and refrigerate.  Mix periodicately during the day. When ready to bake cover a cast iron pan with oil or drippings from the roast ( maybe 2 tbsp, enough to cover the pan nicely) and heat in oven.  Get the oil nice and hot.  Just before baking add the water and mix and pour mixture into hot pan.  Bake for 20 minutes or until puffy and browned, at 400 degrees.
