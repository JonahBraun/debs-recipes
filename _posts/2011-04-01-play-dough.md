Play Dough

- 1/2 c salt
- 2 T cooking oil
- 2 c boiling water
- food coloring
- 2 to 3 c flour
- 1 T alum or 2 T cream of tartar

Mix salt and cooking oil together. Add boiling water and food coloring. Mix in flour and alum or cream of tartar. Knead to dough consistency.
