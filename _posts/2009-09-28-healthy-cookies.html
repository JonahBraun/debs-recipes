--- layout: recipe title: Healthy Cookies category: desserts ---

1 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup unbleached white flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups oatmeal (small flake)
2 tsp. cinamon
1 heaping tablespoon almond flour (optional)
1 heaping tablespoon ground flax seed (optional)

1 egg
1/2 cup honey (generous)
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tbs blackstrap molasses
1 tsp vanilla

1 cup raisons
1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts

Stir dry ingredients together.  Whisk liquids together then add to dry ingredients.  Fold nuts and raisons into batter.  Spoon heaping tablespoons onto cookie sheets.  Bake 10 - 13 minutes at 350 degrees.
