--- layout: recipe title: Beef Curry category: beef --- Simone's favorite beef curry recipe. - 2 lb chuck steak - 1 T lemongrass - ½ t chopped galanga - 6 cloves garlic - 2 t ground black pepper - 1 large chopped onion - 2 t cumin - 1 t blachan - 2 t salt - 8 oz Coconut milk - 2 T ground coriander - 3 c water - ½ t chili powder Place all of the spices in a mortar bowl with the lemon grass, garlic, onion and blachan. Pound to a paste and set aside. Put two thirds of the coconut milk into a pan, add the water and bring to a boil. Cut steak into cubes, add to the coconut milk mixture. Cover and simmer for 1 hour. Heat the remaining coconut milk in a wok. Add the spice paste and dry fry gently for 5 minutes. Drain the meat, reserving the liquid, add the meat to the spices and coconut pan and fry over a high heat for 5 minutes. Add the reserved liquid and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
