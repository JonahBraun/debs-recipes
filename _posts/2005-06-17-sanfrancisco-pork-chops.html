--- layout: recipe title: SanFrancisco Pork Chops category: pork --- Quick chopsl with rich oriental flavor.  serve with a pasta or rice.
  • 3 pork chops 
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 tbs. broth 
  • 2 tbs. brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. corn starch 
  • 4 tbs. soy sauce 
  • ¼ tsp. crushed red pepper 
  • 2 tbs. water

Brown pork chops with garlic. Add sauce ingredients. Simmer until done (25 minutes). Serve with pasta or steamed rice.
