--- layout: recipe title: Maple Glazed Lamb category: lamb ---
 3 kg leg of lamb
2 tbs vegetable oil
 2 tbs hot mustard
 1 tbs lemon juice
 1/3 cup maple syrup
 

Trim excess fat from lamb.  Rub lamb with oil, then mustard.  Barbecue allowing 30 minutes per 500 g.  Base with lemon juice and maple syrup.
