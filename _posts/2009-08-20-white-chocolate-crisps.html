--- layout: recipe title: White Chocolate Crisps category: desserts ---

1 c shortening

1/2 c sugar

2 tbs milk

1/3 c wheat and oat flour

1/2 tsp salt

1 1/2 c rice crispies

3/4 c brown sugar

1 egg

1 1/2 tsp vanilla

1 tsp baking soda

6 squares white chocolate coarsely chopped

 

Cream shortening, sugars, egg, milk and vanilla in large bowl until light and creamy.  Combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Add to creamed mixture, beating at low speed until blended.  Stir in chocolate and cereal.  Mix well.  Drop dough by heaping spoonfuls unto ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 350 F for 10 minutes.
