--- layout: recipe title: Anne's Shortcake category: desserts ---

1/2 c unsalted butter

3 eggs

1 tsp salt

1 c milk

1 1/2 c white sugar

2 c flour

3 1/2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp vanilla

 

Mix dry ingredients together.  In separate bowl blend butter and sugar.  Add eggs.  Add flour mixture alternately with milk and vanilla.  Beat on low 30 seconds and on high for 3 minutes.  Drop by spoonfuls or desired shape on cookie sheet.  Bake for 30 - 35 minutes at 350 F.
