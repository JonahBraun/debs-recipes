--- layout: recipe title: Spicy Oatmeal Cookies category: desserts ---

1 cup raisons
1 tsp salt
 2/3 cup unsalted butter 1 tsp baking powder
 3/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
 2 eggs, beaten
1/2 tsp cinnamon
 1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp nutmeg
 1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp alspice
 1 cup whole wheat flour 2 1/2 cups quick cooking rolled oats
 1 cup white unbleached flour

 

Cream butter and sugar. Stir in eggs, milk, vanilla, and raisins.  Sift together flour, soda, salt, baking powder and spices.  Add creamed mixture and rolled oats.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an oiled baking sheet.  Flatten slightly with a fork dipped in milk if desired.  Bake at 350 for 12 - 15 minutes until slightly browned.

 
  
Note:  These cookies aren't overly sweet.  The secret to the great flavor is using real butter rather than shortening, and the generous use of spices.
