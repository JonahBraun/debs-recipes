--- layout: recipe title: Greek Salad Dressing category: salads --- This dressing is by far the best Greek Dressing I've tasted. 
 4 tsb lemon juice
1 tsp dried oregano
4 tbs red wine vinegar
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
 1/2 c olive oil
1/4 tsp pepper
 1/4 c crumbled feta cheese

Place all ingrediants in a blender except for olive oil and feta cheese.  With the blender running, slowly add olive oil. This is important to do to properly mix the oil with the dressing. After well mixed, add feta cheese.
